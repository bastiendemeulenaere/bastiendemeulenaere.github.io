Introduction

This blog post is a special one for me. It represents something I've always wanted to do and dreamed of. Doing an Erasmus exchange was a goal I set for myself when I started my studies at Howest. I wanted to experience a different culture, meet new people, and challenge myself academically. In this post, I'll share my experiences and insights from my Erasmus exchange.

Conclusion

My Erasmus exchange was an incredible experience that allowed me to immerse myself in a new culture, meet amazing people, and grow both personally and academically. I highly recommend anyone considering an Erasmus exchange to take the leap and go for it. The memories and skills you gain will last a lifetime.
